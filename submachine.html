The Submachine Game Series


Go Back
  • Polish artist Mateusz Skutnik is the creator of Submachine and a few other games (Daymare Town, Covert Front, Slice of Sea, etc), all of which have been dear to me since I was a child.
  • They are browser games, with high-res executables made available for sale later.
  • The games' main points of interest are:
    • The signature atmospheric artstyle and music
    • The labyrinth - a world with hundreds of rooms, full of machines and mechanisms that need to be solved in order to reach the end room, which is the entrance to yet another labyrinth
  • In Submachine, the setting is sci-fi, with the mechanisms going from simple gears and levers to space warpers and teleportators.
  • Originally built by humans, the infrastructure of the Submachine started growing itself and has become impossibly large.
  • You play as a silent protagonist and are trying to escape it.
